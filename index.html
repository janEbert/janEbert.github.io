Hey, I’m Jan (or janEbert on GitHub)! I like optimizing loops, thinking in parallel, applying mathematics, and hardcore challenges.

For some years, I’ve been heavily invested in deep learning; my favorite projects include:

  • Super Mario World level generation framework
  • Reinforcement learning for Durak
  • Generative adversarial network framework

As I have noticed some interest in the project, I am currently working on a rewrite of the Durak reinforcement learning environment in Rust. This will result in a high-performance Gym environment striking the fine balance between a relatively simple but reward-sparse reinforcement learning task. An optimal agent needs to plan for the future to obtain the delayed win-or-lose reward.
Aside from that, Durak is also really fun!
